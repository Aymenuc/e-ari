# E-ARI ad — shot list

Two cuts in this brief: a **60-second hero spot** and a **30-second short** for paid social.

Conventions:
- All asset paths are relative to this `/press-kit/` folder.
- "FG plate" = spokesperson green-screen / keyed in front.
- "BG" = full-frame background, screen-only, no person.
- "PiP" = picture-in-picture overlay.
- Camera moves are slow — *no* whip pans, *no* zooms, *no* drone shots.

## Music

- Minimal piano motif, sustained low pad underneath.
- No drops. No build to a hard stop.
- 65–75 BPM, key Bb minor or D minor.
- Reference: *Hans Zimmer — "Time" (Inception)* but at half-volume and stripped of the brass.

## Spokesperson

- **Casting**: 35–45 years old, ungendered casting OK, enterprise-architect demeanour. Calm, precise, the kind of person who reads regulations for fun.
- **Wardrobe**: navy turtleneck or charcoal merino crewneck. **No** suit jacket, **no** logos.
- **Hair / makeup**: minimal, natural, slight matte powder.
- **Studio**: matte navy backdrop (`#0a0e1a` ± 1 stop). Single key light camera-left at 45°, soft rim light camera-right behind shoulder. **No** background gradient lights, **no** practicals in shot.

## Camera

- Single 50mm-equivalent lens, full-frame sensor look (cinematic DoF, ~f/2.8).
- Locked-off tripod for talking-head shots.
- Slow dolly only when transitioning between screens — never on the spokesperson.
