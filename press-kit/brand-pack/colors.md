# E-ARI brand colors

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| **Navy 950** (background) | `#0a0e1a` | Primary canvas — site bg, video letterboxing |
| **Navy 900** (surface) | `#0f1729` | Cards, panels |
| **Navy 800** (elevated) | `#1e2a45` | Hover states, dividers |
| **Eari Blue – Deep** | `#1e40af` | "Foundation" tier — bottom of progression |
| **Eari Blue – Brand** | `#3b82f6` | Primary CTA, brand accent |
| **Eari Blue – Light** | `#60a5fa` | "Pacesetter" tier, italic accents, links |
| **Foreground** | `#f1f5f9` | Body text on dark |
| **Muted** | `#94a3b8` | Secondary text, mono labels |

## Color grade (for the video editor)

- Shadows: cool teal, lift `#0a0e1a → #0c1428`
- Mids: neutral, no warmth
- Highlights: faint blue cast `#3b82f6` at 8% opacity
- No saturation push above neutral. **No teal-orange.** No film grain.

The brand is *governance-grade*, not *gaming trailer*.
