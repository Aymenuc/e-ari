# E-ARI brand voice (for spokesperson script + VO)

Three rules. They override anything else.

1. **Governance-grade, not enterprise-bland.** We talk to DPOs, internal auditors, AI programme leads — people who get fired if they're wrong. So: precise, specific, citable. Never fluffy, never aspirational without evidence.

2. **Technical without jargon.** "Article 27 FRIA" is fine — that's the actual regulatory artefact and our audience knows the term. "Synergistic AI-enabled value chain transformation" is not fine.

3. **Confident, not promotional.** We don't say "best-in-class" or "industry-leading". We say what the platform does and let the specificity carry the weight.

## Words to use

defensible · auditable · clause-level · evidence-mapped · risk-classified · versioned · reproducible · traceable

## Words to avoid

revolutionary · seamless · cutting-edge · game-changing · synergy · empowers · transforms · unlock (verb) · journey · ecosystem

## Voice samples

✅ *"Eight pillars, forty questions, fifteen minutes. Then six AI agents turn the score into evidence."*

✅ *"Every classification cites the exact article. If a regulator pushes back, you know which clause to point at."*

❌ *"E-ARI revolutionises your AI compliance journey through cutting-edge agentic intelligence."*

❌ *"Empower your team to seamlessly transform readiness into competitive advantage."*

## Pace (for the actor / VO talent)

- 145–160 wpm — slower than a podcast, faster than a TED talk.
- Pauses on numbers. *"Eight … pillars."*
- Never trail off. Each sentence lands.
